HOW IT WORKS

To launch Synchro, simply click the launcher icon at the top of the Chrome Browser. It will then open an unobtrusive in-browser popup (not a separate tab or popout). This is unique to Synchro. Users can then write their task in the task text box, then add the task. With just these 2 clicks, you can start managing your tasks better.

DRAG AND DROP

Synchro features a clean and visually effective Drag and Drop orginaization board in which users can drag and drop their created tasks from "To Do" to "In Progress" to "Completed!". Rather than just a laundry list of tasks, users can easily identify the state of their tasks.

STAY UP TO DATE

Part of being a good organizer, not only must one know what tasks they have to do, but they must be able to do so in a timely manner. Syncho includes a due date functionality, where users can set the date in which need to finish their specific task. Syncho will then automatically change the color of the task from green, to yellow, to red as the task nears its due date. This is revolutionary for prioritization.
